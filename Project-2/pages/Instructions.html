Instructions

Steps Instructions Pictures
1 Wash and pill the Cabbage, chop the green onions

2 Prepare pilled Shrimp

3 Preheat the Corn Oil

4 Add Garlic

5 Add Shrimp

6 Add Cooking Wine

7 Add water, wait until boil

8 Add Whole Wheat Ramen

8 Cook for ten minutes, remove ramen from pot, adding few drops of Meggi and Light Soy Sauce, let the noodle rest.

9 Add Cabbage and more water. Cook for five minutes, carefully stir to help cabbage separate.

9 Remove shrimp and cabbage from pot, carefully adding them on top of the seasoned ramen. Adding some Green Onions on top of the dish. And you are done!
